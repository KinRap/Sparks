Director: Lasse Hallstrom

Cast: Channing Tatum, Amanda Seyfried

"Dear John" is a romantic drama film directed by Lasse Hallstr√∂m and based on the novel of the same name by Nicholas Sparks. The movie stars Channing Tatum as John Tyree, a soldier on leave, and Amanda Seyfried as Savannah Curtis, a college student he falls in love with during her spring vacation.

The story follows John and Savannah as they navigate their relationship over the course of seven years, during which they are separated by John's military deployments. Despite the distance, they maintain their connection through a continuous exchange of love letters. However, their relationship faces challenges and unforeseen consequences as they struggle to reconcile their love with the realities of John's service in the military.
